About This Speargun:

Built for seasoned spearfishers, the Poseidon Hunter Pro delivers unrelenting force and tactical precision beneath the waves. Designed for strong currents and deep dives, this speargun is a masterclass in engineering and power, perfect for those who seek total control in the ocean's toughest environments.

Materials:

  • High-tensile marine-grade stainless steel trigger system
  • Dense Indonesian teakwood barrel for stability and strength

  • Polyurethane-coated handle for enhanced grip and durability

Key Features:

  • Precision-balanced for seamless tracking in open water
  • Quick-release trigger mechanism for instant reaction time

  • Low-drag frame design for agile underwater handling
  • Optimized band placement for maximum power output
